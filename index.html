MY LITTLE RANT ABOUT MODERN WEBSITES :)


There is nothing on the internet that frustrates me more than these super-modern, flashy, animaty, samey websites with that stupid "Learn More" button. Just give me everything in plain sight, nice and straightforward - not just visually, but programmatically as well. I like websites that are ridiculously simple, and have most of the information in as little screen space as possible. I don't want to scroll through flashy transition animations and oversized web assets. Modern websites are stupid af. Just a bunch of bloat... in my opinion.
